What is PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana? +
PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana (PMSGY) is a government scheme launched by PM Narendra Modi in February 2024 to promote rooftop solar installations for residential households. ecozen is an empaneled vendor under this scheme, helping homeowners get solar installed with maximum subsidy support.
How much subsidy can I get? +
Under PMSGY: Up to 2kW — ₹30,000 per kW (60% subsidy). For the 3rd kW — ₹18,000. Maximum central subsidy is ₹78,000. UP state offers an additional ₹30,000 subsidy. Subsidy is transferred directly to your bank account after installation.
Is ecozen an approved vendor under PMSGY? +
Yes — ecozen is an empaneled vendor under PM Surya Ghar Yojana. Our certified dealers help you with end-to-end support — from portal registration to installation to subsidy disbursement.
What's included in ecozen's full solar kit? +
ecozen provides a complete solar kit including Solar Panels (from trusted brands), On-Grid Inverter, and Heavy-duty Hot Dip Galvanized Solar Structure. All components come with warranties — panels (25yr), inverter (7yr), structure (5yr).
How long does installation take? +
Installation is typically completed within 5–7 days after PMSGY approval. The entire process — from booking to installation — is supported by ecozen's certified dealer network.
