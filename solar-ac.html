What makes ecozen Solar AC different from regular AC? +
ecozen Solar AC includes a patented AI Solar Controller that intelligently switches between solar, battery, and grid power — keeping your AC running 24/7 even during power cuts. Regular ACs stop when the grid goes down. ecozen doesn't.
Does it work without solar panels? +
Yes — ecozen Solar AC works on grid power too. But adding solar panels means you can cool your home for free using solar energy, dramatically reducing your electricity bills.
Does it work during power cuts? +
Yes — with battery backup, ecozen Solar AC continues to run even when the grid power goes out. The AI controller switches to battery power automatically — no interruption in cooling.
What is the AI Solar Controller? +
The AI Solar Controller is ecozen's patented device that acts as the brain of the system. It monitors solar generation in real-time, adjusts AC capacity based on available solar power, switches between power sources seamlessly, and prevents tripping — ensuring continuous, efficient cooling.
