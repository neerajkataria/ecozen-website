How does Ecotron work? +
Ecotron connects to your solar irrigation system, enabling remote monitoring and control via the app. It tracks water usage, provides real-time data, and ensures efficient, cost-effective irrigation.

Subsidy Amounts — Up to 2 kW: ₹30,000 per kW · Between 2 kW and 3 kW: ₹18,000 per additional kW · Above 3 kW: Capped at ₹78,000. UP Government's additional subsidy: ₹15,000 per kW up to ₹30,000.
