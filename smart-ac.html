Does ecozen Smart AC work without solar panels? +
Yes — ecozen Smart AC works on both solar power and regular grid electricity. You can use it even without solar panels, but connecting solar maximizes savings.
What app do I need to control the AC? +
ecozen Smart AC is controlled via the EcoSphere™ app, available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Play Store). The app lets you monitor temperature, set schedules, raise service requests, and more.
What is the warranty on ecozen Smart AC? +
ecozen Smart AC comes with 1 year comprehensive warranty + 5 years on the compressor — one of the best warranty packages in the industry.
What sizes are available? +
ecozen Smart AC is available in 1.0 Ton, 1.5 Ton, and 2.0 Ton variants to suit different room sizes and requirements.
