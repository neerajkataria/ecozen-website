Common questions answered
How does ecofrost work? +
ecofrost uses patented Ice-Core Technology — it stores cooling energy as ice during daytime (when solar is available) and releases it gradually throughout the night, ensuring 24/7 cold storage without continuous power dependency.
What can I store in ecofrost? +
ecofrost is ideal for fruits, flowers, vegetables, dairy products, and dry commodities. The 4–13°C cooling range suits a wide variety of perishables commonly grown in India.
Does ecofrost work without solar? +
Yes. ecofrost works seamlessly on both solar and grid power. It can operate on solar during the day, store cooling energy, and continue operating overnight — or switch to grid when needed.
Is ecofrost portable? +
Yes — ecofrost is designed to be portable. It requires no civil work or permanent installation, making it easy to relocate between farms or distribution points as needed.
